## Long description — example

> **Enjoy the love, let Entie handle the rest.**
>
> Entie is a private space for couples. It quietly keeps track of what's going on — moods, cycles, the rhythm of your week — and gives both of you small, useful nudges that make showing up for each other easier.
>
> **What you can do together:**
>
> 🌿 **Track the cycle, together.** Either one of you can log it. Both of you get the picture. The partner gets daily tips on how to show up that day.
>
> ☀️ **A daily read on your relationship.** Entie writes a short summary every day — what's likely on her mind, one small thing you could do about it.
>
> 💬 **Three ways to talk.** Ask Entie anything in Simple Chat. Work through something confusing in Deep Talk. Have the hard conversation together in Guided Space, with a quiet AI presence in the room.
>
> 🌀 **Questions you'd never bring up otherwise.** Couple Questions reveals both answers only when both of you have answered. Submit your own — Entie finds the right moment.
>
> 🌸 **Built for every stage.** Cycle, pregnancy, or menopause — Entie adapts to where you are in life.
>
> 🔒 **Private by design.** Your data is yours. Privacy isn't a feature, it's the whole point.
>
> Entie is for couples aged 26–44 who want to feel close again — or stay close as life changes. Any configuration, any age, any stage.
