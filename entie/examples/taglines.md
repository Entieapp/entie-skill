## Lines to avoid (examples of what *not* to write)

These are anti-patterns. They sound like Entie at first glance but break the brand:

- ❌ "Revolutionize your relationship." — too bombastic
- ❌ "Hack your love life." — wrong register, productivity framing
- ❌ "The Notion for couples." — defining by another app
- ❌ "Built for modern lovers who demand more." — generic SaaS speak
- ❌ "Fix what's broken between you." — implies the couple is broken
- ❌ "Spice things up 🔥" — coy/suggestive (see [`../brand/guardrails.md`](../brand/guardrails.md))
