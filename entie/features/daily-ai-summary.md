# Feature: Daily AI Summary

## What it is

Each day, both partners see a personalized AI-generated summary of what's going on in the relationship that day. It's the daily briefing.

## How it works

- The AI pulls together everything logged: cycle day, mood, recent activity, sexual activity, anything else the woman has noted.
- It generates a brief recap of the day's context.
- For the partner, it includes **recommendations** — likely scenarios for the day and how to navigate them ("she may be tired today; a quiet evening would land well").
- Both partners see the summary. There is no hidden version.

## Who uses it

- **Both partners** — same summary, both can act on it.

## Why it matters

People want to show up well for their partner but often don't have the context to do it. The daily summary closes that gap. It turns the question *"how are they doing today?"* from a guess into a briefing.

It also normalizes the act of checking in — instead of asking the woman to explain her state, the partner already has a starting point.

## How to talk about it

### Lead with

- The everyday utility — this is a tool used every day, not occasionally.
- The recommendation aspect for the partner ("Entie tells you what's likely going on and what to do about it").
- Shared visibility — both partners see the same thing.

### Avoid

- Sounding like a productivity dashboard
- "Insights" and "analytics" framing — keep it human
- Promising perfect predictions

### Sample lines

- *"Wake up. Open Entie. Know what kind of day today is."*
- *"A short daily read. A better evening together."*
- *"What's likely on her mind today — and one small thing you could do about it."*

## Edge cases worth knowing

- The summary is generated by AI and may occasionally be off-base. Never frame it as authoritative — it's a starting point.
- The summary respects whatever life-stage mode the woman is in (Cycle / Pregnancy / Menopause).
- Days with no logged data may produce shorter or more generic summaries.
