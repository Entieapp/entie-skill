# Position Statement

Entie serves people in a romantic relationship by providing a space that makes them confident in their connection. Entie is unique because it values privacy and personal needs, giving couples quick, actionable recommendations on what to do next to maintain — or even improve — their relationship.

## In one sentence

**Entie is the AI co-pilot for modern couples.**

## What this means for content

- Lead with the *relationship*, not the app. The app is the means; the relationship is the point.
- "Confidence" is a useful word — Entie helps couples feel sure of themselves and each other.
- Privacy and personalization are not nice-to-haves; they are reasons Entie exists.
- Recommendations are *quick* and *actionable*. Avoid framing Entie as a slow, reflective tool — it is responsive and present in daily life.
