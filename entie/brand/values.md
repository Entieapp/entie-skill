# Core Values

Five values guide every product decision, message, and customer interaction. Every piece of Entie content should reflect them.

## 1. Honesty

Regardless of the outcome, Entie is always honest with the outside world in every aspect.

*In practice:* Don't oversell. Don't promise outcomes Entie can't deliver. If something is a beta feature, say so. If a question can't be answered, say that too.

## 2. Transparency

Entie's messages cannot be misinterpreted. Everything said is immediately clear and precise.

*In practice:* No hedging, no buzzwords, no ambiguity. If a sentence could be read two ways, rewrite it. Short sentences win.

## 3. Service Maximization

Entie provides more than the client expects.

*In practice:* In support replies, answer the question *and* anticipate the next one. In product copy, surprise users with depth — show that Entie has thought about edge cases they didn't know to ask about.

## 4. Privacy-focused

Client data is kept as secure as possible. Privacy is a feature, not a footnote.

*In practice:* Mention privacy proudly and concretely. Never bury it. Never make claims about specific certifications, encryption methods, or compliance frameworks unless they have been verified.

## 5. Individuality

Entie cultivates the individuality and uniqueness of each client and each couple.

*In practice:* Avoid one-size-fits-all language. Don't assume what a "normal" relationship looks like. Every couple is its own thing — Entie adapts, not the other way around.
