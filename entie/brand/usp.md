# Unique Selling Proposition

> **Enjoy the love, let Entie handle the rest.**

## What it captures

Couples focus on each other. Entie handles the noticing, remembering, suggesting, and structuring.

## When to use it

- Hero sections of the website and App Store
- Brand-level moments in ad creatives
- Headlines of major campaigns
- The closing line of a brand video or pitch

## When *not* to use it

- Inside support replies — too marketing-toned for customer service
- In feature explanations — the USP is brand-level, not feature-level
- In every other sentence — it loses weight when overused

## Don't dilute it

Unless explicitly asked for variations, keep the line exactly as written: *Enjoy the love, let Entie handle the rest.*

When variations are requested (for A/B tests or different ad formats), preserve the structure: **a verb of pleasure aimed at the partner + Entie taking care of everything else.** Examples that hold the spirit:

- "Love freely. Entie's got the rest."
- "Be present. Let Entie remember."
- "All you have to do is love them."

Avoid variations that put the work back on the couple, get clinical, or sound like a productivity app.
