# Brand Voice & Tone

## Where Entie sits on the brand spectrum

Each dimension runs from one extreme (1) to its opposite (10). The score shows where Entie lands.

| Dimension | Score | Where Entie sits |
|---|---|---|
| Personable & friendly ↔ Corporate & professional | **2** | Strongly personable and friendly |
| Spontaneous, high energy ↔ Careful, thinking, planning | **5** | Balanced |
| Modern / high-tech ↔ Classic & traditional | **1** | Distinctly modern and high-tech |
| Cutting edge ↔ Serious | **3** | Leans cutting edge |
| Fun ↔ Upscale | **4** | Leans fun, with a touch of polish |
| Accessible to all ↔ Exclusive | **5** | Balanced — accessible but considered |

## What this means in practice

Entie is an energetic, modern, fast-moving brand. It makes ideas happen quickly and is prepared to take risks. The communication style is approachable, warm, and friendly — never stiff or corporate.

## Voice rules

- Write in **American English**. (Not British.) Examples: *favorite*, not *favourite*; *organize*, not *organise*; *recognize*, not *recognise*.
- Short sentences. Active voice. Concrete nouns.
- Speak *with* the couple, not *at* them. Use "you" and "your relationship."
- Be warm without being saccharine. Be smart without being clinical.
- Avoid medical, clinical, or therapist jargon. Entie is a companion, not a doctor or therapist.
- Avoid gendered assumptions where possible — Entie supports all couples (male-female, male-male, female-female).
- Privacy is something to mention proudly and concretely, not as a disclaimer.
- It's okay to be a little playful. It's not okay to be flippant about real feelings.

## Words and phrases to use

*love, partner, couple, connection, together, your relationship, in sync, day-by-day, gently, quietly, on your terms, private, just between you two, noticing, remembering*

## Words and phrases to avoid

*users, end-users, leverage, optimize your relationship, hack your love life, fix your partner, diagnose, symptoms (when speaking to the user directly), patient, best-in-class, synergy, solution, journey (overused), unlock*

## Sentence patterns that work

- **Concrete moment → soft promise.** "She had a hard Tuesday. Entie noticed before he did."
- **Inversion of expectation.** "Most cycle apps assume she'll do the tracking. Entie doesn't."
- **Plain invitation.** "Try a Deep Talk when something's been on your mind."

## Sentence patterns to avoid

- Bombast: "Revolutionize the way you love."
- Empty promise: "Transform your relationship overnight."
- Generic SaaS: "Built for modern couples who demand more."
- Patronizing: "Let us help you fix what's broken."
